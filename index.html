Gantry Crane – STARKON 10 Ton

This robust new STARKON single-girder gantry crane is suitable for various industrial applications, both indoors and outdoors. The crane is equipped with a high-quality wire rope hoist from Verlinde and is built to FEM/ISO class M5 standards – ideal for intensive use. With a capacity of 10 tons and a lifting height of 4.2 meters, this crane offers a powerful and reliable solution for all your lifting operations. Includes 12 meters of crane runway (height 40 mm), with adjustable length as desired.
